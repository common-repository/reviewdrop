Reviewdrop adds a widget to your website for collecting and displaying reviews about your business. Let customers submit text, image, video, twitter and instagram posts then offer discount codes as a thank you.

== Description ==

Reviewdrop helps grow your online business by allowing customers to submit and view visually rich reviews. Anything from text, image, video, twitter to instagram posts.

= Your first 5 reviews for free =

This plugin requires you to sign up to [Reviewdrop](https://reviewdrop.io). You can start completely free for your first 5 reviews. Paid plans start at only £5/pm for 30 reviews or £15/pm for unlimited review submissions.

= Woocommerce Conversion Review Invites = 
Automatically invite customers to leave a review after they make an order. You can set to send email invitations any number of days after the order to ensure the customer has the product delivered first.

== Installation ==

To install Reviewdrop on WordPress you first need to have an account with [Reviewdrop](https://reviewdrop.io).

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/reviewdrop` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Use the Settings->Reviewdrop screen to configure the plugin.
4. Navigate to your Reviewdrop dashboard and click the [Install](https://reviewdrop.io/embed) page on the left sidebar.
5. Copy the 'ID' and paste into the 'ID' field on the WordPress options screen.
6. Customise the widget as you desire and select 'Save changes'
7. Done! Your widget will now appear across your website.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do you compare with other review services? =

Our goal with Reviewdrop is to provide a content rich experience for your customers to submit in many forms - such as, text, images, video, instagram or twitter posts. Coupon codes on submission also provide an extra layer of incentive to let your customers write reviews.

= Can I delete reviews? =

To keep things fair for both your business and customers, you cannot delete reviews submitted by a user. Posts imported from Instagram/Twitter however can be as they are not included as part of your average review rating.
